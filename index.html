Steps to Reach Goal (Action Plan)

達成目標的步驟 (行動清單)

  • Year 1-2 (Entry-Level/Technician): Solidify L2/L3 support skills. Complete CDCP (Certified Data Centre Professional) certification. Seek opportunities to lead small internal infrastructure upgrade projects.
  • Year 3-5 (Senior Engineer/Supervisor): Achieve ITIL 4 Foundation or ITIL Expert certification to master service management frameworks. Focus on learning DCIM (Data Center Infrastructure Management) tools for reporting and efficiency analysis.
  • Year 5+ (Managerial Focus): Obtain PMP (Project Management Professional) or equivalent management certification. Actively participate in budget planning and vendor negotiation processes.
  • Networking & Development: Regularly attend industry conferences (e.g., DCD Events) and join professional bodies like the Infrastructure Masons to expand domain knowledge and leadership connections.
  • 第 1-2 年 (初級/技術人員): 鞏固 L2/L3 支援技能。完成 CDCP（註冊數據中心專業人員）認證。尋求機會領導小型內部基礎設施升級項目。
  • 第 3-5 年 (高級工程師/主管): 考取 ITIL 4 Foundation 或 ITIL Expert 認證，以掌握服務管理框架。專注於學習 DCIM（數據中心基礎設施管理）工具，以進行報告和效率分析。
  • 第 5 年以上 (管理重點): 獲得 PMP（項目管理專業人士）或同等管理認證。積極參與預算規劃和供應商談判過程。
  • 人脈建立與發展: 定期參加行業會議（例如：DCD Events）並加入專業機構，如 Infrastructure Masons，以擴展領域知識和領導力人脈。

Necessary Skills to Learn & Plan

需學習的技能與計劃

  • Advanced Network & Security: Master BGP, OSPF, and advanced firewall policies. *Plan: Complete a Cisco Certified Network Professional (CCNP) course within 2 years.*
  • Data Center Infrastructure Management (DCIM): Hands-on experience with commercial DCIM tools (e.g., Schneider Electric EcoStruxure, Nlyte) for capacity planning and energy efficiency (PUE) monitoring. *Plan: Complete 3 DCIM vendor-specific training modules within 18 months.*
  • HVAC, Power, and Critical Systems: Deepen knowledge of UPS, generators, cooling methodologies (e.g., liquid cooling trends), and facility maintenance best practices. *Plan: Audit 2 relevant facilities management courses within the next year.*
  • IT Service Management (ITSM): Apply the ITIL framework to standardize incident, change, and capacity management. *Plan: ITIL 4 Foundation certification within 1 year.*
  • 高級網絡與安全: 掌握 BGP、OSPF 和高級防火牆策略。*計劃: 在 2 年內完成 Cisco 認證網絡專業人員 (CCNP) 課程。*
  • 數據中心基礎設施管理 (DCIM): 掌握商業 DCIM 工具（例如：Schneider Electric EcoStruxure、Nlyte）的實戰經驗，用於容量規劃和能源效率 (PUE) 監控。*計劃: 在 18 個月內完成 3 個 DCIM 供應商專門的培訓模塊。*
  • 暖通空調、電力和關鍵系統: 深化對 UPS、發電機、冷卻方法（例如：液體冷卻趨勢）和設施維護最佳實踐的知識。*計劃: 在未來一年內旁聽 2 門相關的設施管理課程。*
  • IT 服務管理 (ITSM): 應用 ITIL 框架來標準化事件、變更和容量管理。*計劃: 在 1 年內考取 ITIL 4 Foundation 認證。*
